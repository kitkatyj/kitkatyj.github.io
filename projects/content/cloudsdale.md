# The Cloudsdale Splash Launch Trailer

>"The whole world will be up tonight. Gonna party, Equestria. Party, Equestria. Until Celestia raises the sun." St. Pinkie, Party Equestria, 2014

The Cloudsdale Splash was an art festival held in Singapore organised by St. Pinkie that aimed to gather like-minded fans of the show My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, which had a growing artistic community. The show has garnered a sizeable audience in the United States with conventions garnering thousands of attendees. Southeast Asia isn't going to be left behind, and therefore this event was made!

I was asked by St. Pinkie, one of the organisers and who has also composed the music, to create a short animated promotional video. This was where I got to practice my motion graphics and animation skills using Adobe After Effects, which I was starting to get familiar with. It was released 1.5 months before the event itself. This also marked a beginning for my journey of learning Motion Graphics and producing content for the community.

The event was eventually held on 20-21 December 2014, with art jamming sessions, a rock concert, and a tie in music album release. Around 50 people had attended the event.

For more information about the My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic community (a.k.a. Brony community), visit [WhatIsABrony.com](https://www.whatisabrony.com) for the understanding of the fandom's history and future.
