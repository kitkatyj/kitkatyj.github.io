# Flurry's Part

>"BUY OUR TOYS. BUY OUR TOYS. BUY OUR TOYS." [Sawtooth Waves, 2016](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BwecDWBZ9Vk)

## Flurry and Waves of Emotions
From the success of [[anpow|A New Pair of Wings]], and the recent ending of Generation 4 of My Little Pony, Sawtooth Waves returned to me to create a brand new music video for another song they have written, this time for the alicorn Flurry Heart. Unlike Starlight Glimmer who was granted wings from Twilight Sparkle in the previous music video, Flurry Heart was born with wings in canon, which opened up a whole can of worms within the fandom.

Although never expanded upon in canon, it was always an interesting thought experiment to see how Flurry Heart might harbour similar feelings to that of Starlight Glimmer if she also had wings that were not deserved. That is the premise of the new song.

## In Good Faith
Sawtooth approached me again in May 2020 with the new song they made and requested if I could make visuals for it as well. They were in particular inspired by [Madeon's Good Faith](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL9tY0BWXOZFstkNOduQRQlgpn-DJ8om2a) visuals, and wanted to carry that aesthetic into the music video. I, am actually quite a big fan of Madeon who have listened to his music since his collab with Porter Robinson in Shelter.

The aesthetics in Good Faith is certainly pretty interesting. Mapping what is a full colour palette restricted into just 3-4 colours. These colours I have then picked from Flurry Heart's design and accentuated them, such that it spills into the rest of the visuals. I have been told that it looks like the colours of the transgender flag, and I have to agree lol. It's pretty cool.

I was incredibly pumped with this visual: 
![princesses-cutie-marks.png](/img/princesses-cutie-marks.png)

Once again, it was a bunch of back and forth working on the visuals while Sawtooth was finishing the song and having Vylet reprising her vocal role once again.

## Release
The music video was released on 1 January 2021, to similar fanfare to 'A New Pair of Wings'. I eventually uploaded the 4K 60fps version of the music video a couple months later on 20 March 2021.

## Author's Note
I do not condone the actions which had come to light from Sawtooth in February 2023, and no longer keep in contact with them since. They have since removed all content from their YouTube channel, including the original upload of "Flurry's Part" which has been marked as "private". I therefore would like to claim the 4K 60fps version uploaded on daspacepony as the official version.
