# A New Pair of Wings

>*"It is said that during the Total Solar Eclipse, Starlight will shine."* me, 2017

## A Notion to Fly
In 2017, I was recommended by a friend of mine to work together with Sawtooth Waves (then known as The Brony Notion) to produce a fully animated music video to their original song "A New Pair of Wings", about a well-beloved character in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic called Starlight Glimmer.

The song explores a storyline in which the unicorn Starlight Glimmer is unbeknownst, granted a pair of wings and her ascension to an alicorn, just as her mentor Twilight Sparkle did. However, Starlight does not take the news of it lightly, as she believes she is not worthy of her new title, which is represented by her new wings. She is eventually comforted by the notion that she is in fact not alone and that alicorns that come before (including her mentor Twilight) will be able to guide her and fly with her to new heights as she steps into the new role. 

## Visual Concepts
The theme of flying and wings is central to the song, especially with the flapping sound effects in the intro. I was then asked to produce animated wings to accompany that sound effect. Sawtooth's request to make this something bigger than just a visualiser and is dramatic. I have enlisted help from the friend who recommended me to Sawtooth to produce some custom character animations as that was an area I do not have much experience in.

Perhaps the highlight moment of the music video is after the final drop, where Starlight blasts into the sky alongside her fellow alicorns and flies in sync with them. Friends back then would typically know me for this style of motion graphics, which involves streaks of lights and particles animated with the music. I love animating this kind of aesthetic as it allows me to play with After Effects expressions using the trigonometry I have learned in school. One day I hope to produce a tutorial that showcases how trigonometry can be used in animation.

## The Great Eclipse
The music video itself took around 4 months starting in May. When the music video was about to be completed, we felt it was appropriate that it release on the day of the [Great American Eclipse of 2017](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_August_21,_2017). It was quite the coincidence in fact, as it is known that during a total solar eclipse, enough sunlight gets blocked that starlight that is usually obscured gets to shine during the day time. I felt that it was quite poetic that we, just like during the eclipse, is giving Starlight a chance to shine.

## Release
The video was officially released on 21 August 2017 to spectacular success, and it grew to be Sawtooth Waves' most popular video, with over 3 million views within a year. 😱

As part of a thank you for the viewership, I uploaded a special edition of the music video in rendered in 4K 60fps as a standalone on my channel, compared to 1080p 24fps in the official version. This marks the first big video I have created in that resolution.

To this day, "A New Pair of Wings" remains my magnum opus in the My Little Pony community, even though it is actually not the [most viewed video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vz9MAmRXhos) I have produced for that community. When I eventually moved on from creating fan content for the show, I still get surprised reactions that I made that music video.
