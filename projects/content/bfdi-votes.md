# BFDI Votes

>*"LET'S PLAY A GAME!"* Four ([BFB 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m_7nnajnaI8)), 2017

Ever since [Battle for Dream Island](https://bfdi.tv) was released, I get incredibly excited to see voting statistics for each character. Especially, after one of the show's creators Cary Huang uploaded [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AdskloIt_ZE) that puts the number of votes for each episode to scale with each other.

I love infographics, and wanted to explore the possibilities of creating my own interactive ones. I was already pretty familiar with the HTML5 Canvas in my previous project [[graph-transformer|Graph Transformer]], but of course the limitation of that is that it's all bitmap drawing. What if I wanted to draw vectors? The led me to the javascript library known as [D3.js](https://d3js.org), which allowed me to manipulate SVGs (Standard Vector Graphics) in fun intuitive ways. 

I just love creating infographics and building the user interfaces that make it interactive, and I was incredibly proud of the result, especially of the little popup that follows the mouse that details the specific votes of each contestant. I do think there's lots of potential for improvement as well, writing this more than 8 years later. 👀
