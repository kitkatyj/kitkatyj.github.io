# Space Unicorn Explores the Solar System

>"As I always say, class... you're out of this world." Ms. Frizzle, [The Magic School Bus - Gets Lots in Space, 1994](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0764077/)

This was a school assignment that involved us using Adobe Animate's CreateJS integration which can be exported into a HTML5 Canvas. All assets are made by me!

My passion for astronomy goes all the way back to when I was 3 years old, when my parents bought me an encyclopaedia, and I just can never get my eyes off the space section. The concept of planets just seem to intrigue me the most, and I was able to recite the planets of the Solar System in order from a very young age.

One of the highlights of my childhood was watching The Magic School Bus, with its episode on the Solar System called ["Gets Lost in Space"](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0764077/), and a subsequent tie in CD game called ["Explores the Solar System"](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt30990693/). It is that particular game that inspired me to make my own version many many years later.

The idea of the space unicorn comes from my YouTube channel daspacepony, which is represented by my original character who is also a unicorn. Also coincidentally, a [popular song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=17o1OlroNSE) by Parry Gripp!
